Who we are?


This website was created by researchers at the University of the West of England (UWE Bristol), with funding from the university. It was designed and built by Nastya Sardanova, a BSc Digital Media student at UWE Bristol.

The research team behind this project is Dr Elisa Covato, Dr Kamran Soomro, and Prof Zaheer Khan, from the UWE Smart Cities research group.


How this came about ?


During the GREENGAGE project , a pan-European initiative supporting communities to collect and use local data to influence environmental policy , we worked closely with community organisations in East Bristol. Through that work, it became clear that many groups wanted to use AI tools in their research and reporting but had no accessible, trustworthy guide to help them do so responsibly.


This website is our response to that gap. It was not part of GREENGAGE itself, but it would not exist without what we learned through it.


How we made it?


We ran a series of workshops with community representatives in East Bristol, facilitated by Practitioners Annali Grimes and Julia Costa Carneiro. Those conversations — about what people already knew, what they wanted to learn, and what concerned them — directly shaped what this website covers and how it is written.
